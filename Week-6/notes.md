# Week 6: The Forge – Sprint Finale

## Overview

Week 6 served as the capstone of The Forge, requiring the integration of skills developed throughout the previous five weeks. Rather than introducing new technologies, the focus shifted to troubleshooting, diagnostics, and full-scale infrastructure deployment. Through break/fix scenarios, practical assessments, and a comprehensive enterprise build, I demonstrated proficiency across Linux, networking, Python, Docker, and Active Directory environments.
