# Week 7: The Perimeter

## Overview

Week 7 marked the transition from infrastructure defense to offensive security and reconnaissance. The focus was on identifying potential attack surfaces through passive intelligence gathering, active network scanning, and vulnerability analysis. Through hands-on assessments, I learned how security professionals discover exposed assets, enumerate services, evaluate vulnerabilities, and prioritize risks based on business impact.

## Skills Developed

* Open-Source Intelligence (OSINT)
* Passive reconnaissance techniques
* Subdomain enumeration
* Technology stack identification
* Credential exposure analysis
* Shodan reconnaissance
* Network discovery and host enumeration
* Nmap scanning and service detection
* Vulnerability scanning with Nikto
* CVE and CVSS analysis
* Risk assessment and prioritization
* Remediation planning
* Security reporting

## Labs Completed

### Day 1: The Invisible Scout

Conducted passive reconnaissance to map an organization's external attack surface using OSINT tools. Identified subdomains, technology stacks, potential credential exposures, and publicly accessible infrastructure without directly interacting with target systems.

**Artifact:** `ThreatProfile_CloudNano.md`

### Day 2: Mapping the Shadows

Performed active network reconnaissance using Nmap to discover live hosts, identify open ports, and enumerate running services within a controlled environment.

**Artifact:** `nmap_scan_results.txt`

### Day 3: The Prioritization Matrix

Executed a vulnerability assessment using Nikto and analyzed scan results to identify the most critical security risks. Applied risk-based decision-making using likelihood and impact rather than relying solely on CVSS scores.

**Artifact:** `remediation_plan.md`

### Day 4: Perimeter Assessment

Conducted a full-scope reconnaissance and vulnerability assessment of a simulated corporate subnet. Documented discovered assets, identified security weaknesses, prioritized findings, and developed recommendations for leadership review.

**Artifact:** `Perimeter_Assessment.md`

## Key Takeaways

This week provided practical experience in the reconnaissance and vulnerability assessment phases of the cyber attack lifecycle. By combining passive intelligence gathering, active scanning, and risk analysis, I developed a stronger understanding of how attackers identify opportunities and how security professionals prioritize remediation efforts based on real-world business risk.
